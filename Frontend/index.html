Classic Cheesecake Recipe

Ingredients

Crust:

  • 2 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 6 tablespoons melted butter

Filling:

  • 32 oz cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 4 eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1/4 cup sour cream

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 325°F
  2. Mix graham cracker crumbs, sugar, and butter. Press into 9-inch springform pan
  3. Beat cream cheese until smooth
  4. Add sugar and beat well
  5. Mix in eggs one at a time, then vanilla and sour cream
  6. Pour filling over crust
  7. Bake 55-60 minutes until center is almost set
  8. Cool completely, then refrigerate 4 hours before serving

Tips

  • Let cream cheese come to room temperature for best results
  • Avoid overmixing to prevent cracks
  • Use a water bath for even baking
